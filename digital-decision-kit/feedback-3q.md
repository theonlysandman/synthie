# Feedback ask (paste these 3 questions under your brief)

When you email your one-page brief back, please add quick answers to these three. It takes two
minutes and it's how this gets better for the next teams.

1. **What was confusing or slow?** (Anything you had to re-read, or where you got stuck.)

2. **Did the recommended use case feel right?** (Would you actually take it to your customer? If not,
   what would you have picked instead?)

3. **Could you see building it on Azure?** (Was the Microsoft build path clear and realistic for a
   first AI project?)

> Optional bonus: how long did the whole thing take you, end to end?
