# Quick start (1 page)

Find the first AI use case worth building for one of your customers — in ~20 minutes. No setup.

## Before you start
Pick **one customer you know well**. That's all you need.

> **Talk, don't type.** Use the microphone / dictation button in your chat app and *speak* your
> answers. You'll say more, and the brief comes out much better. Rambling is welcome.

## The 5 steps

1. **Open Microsoft Copilot** (or ChatGPT / any chat assistant) in a **new chat**.

2. **Paste the Synthie prompt.** Open `synthie-system-prompt.md`, copy everything below the divider
   line, paste it into the chat, and send.

3. **Answer its questions, one at a time.** It asks about your customer first — industry, the person
   you'd help, how they make money, what data they have. **Tap the mic and talk** in your own words;
   ramble freely. *(Optional: did some research? Paste it in after the first question.)*

4. **Confirm, then get your ideas.** It plays back the customer picture and asks "Did I get that
   right?" Say yes (or correct it). It then shows 3–4 AI use cases, scores each 0–5, and recommends
   the **one to build first** on Azure.

5. **Finalize and send.** Type **finalize**. Copy the one-page brief it produces, add the 3 feedback
   notes from `feedback-3q.md`, and **email it back to <Your name / email>**.

## Good to know
- **It favours buildable-now over flashy.** That's intentional — it's tuned for a *first* AI project.
- **Don't know something?** Say so; it marks it as an assumption.
- **Want a different idea?** Say "more options" or "back to profile".
- **Want it Microsoft-only?** Say "make it Microsoft".

## Stuck?
- Jumped to ideas too fast → "back to profile" and add more about the customer.
- Ideas feel generic → tell it more about *where the money moves* and *what data they have*.
